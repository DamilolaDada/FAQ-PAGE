  4. How can I claim my late father’s/mother’s shares

    5. ANS; {link to requirements for share transmission below}

    1. Two (2) copies of Grant of probate/Letters of Administration and original for sighting or the Certified True Copy (CTC) from a Notary Public
      • The Grant of probate/Letters of Administration must list all the shareholdings and other names of the deceased
      • An Amended copy of Letters of Administration is required where one ormore of the Administrators is deceased
    2. Two (2) copies of Death Certificate from the National Population Commission (NPC) and original for sighting
    3. Original Banker’s Confirmation of Signature on the estate account, signed by all the Administrators/Executors
      • Banker’s confirmation of signature with disclaimer/exemption clauses will not be accepted
    4. Two (2) copies of Newspaper Publication/Gazette published by the Court
    5. Letter of Introduction and Request for Transmission of Shares signed by all the Administrators/Executors
    6. Two (2) copies of valid means of identification of the Administrators/Executors and original for sighting
    7. Completed e-dividend forms with signatures and BVNs of individual Administrators/Executors
    8. Any evidence of ownership of the investment i.e. CSCS statement(s) of the deceased, original share certificates, dividend stub or dividend warrants or bank statement(s) showing receipt of dividend(s) into the account(s) of the deceased Fees chargeable for Transmission of Shares shall be calculated as follows:
      • (a) For a value of shares of N5 million and below - 1% of the value +7.5% VAT
      • (b) For a value of shares above N5 million - 0.5% of the value subject to a maximum of N200, 000.00 +7.5% VAT
      • (c) Fees chargeable for confirmation of Grant of Probate/Letter of Administration:
        • - Lagos – N10,000.00
        • - Other States - N12,000.00
        • Transmission fee payment details are as follows:
          • Zenith Bank Plc A/C 1013044768 in favour of Meristem Reg. Ltd/Investment Proceed

  6. How can I access your probate services

    7. Please find below other necessary requirements to commence the task:

    1. Min. of 2 and max of 4 Administrators to bring the following:
      • a) Two passport photographs each
      • b) Valid means of identification (Voter’s Card, National ID Card, Nigerian International Passport or Drivers’ License)
    2. Passport photograph and evidence of assets owned by the deceased (dividend warrant / Share certificate / Bank account number / RSA account number/Property document)
    3. Ten (10%) percent of the value of the Estate is to be paid to the Probate Registry as Estate tax
    4. N7,000 (Seven Thousand Naira) publication fee also to be paid to the Probate Registry
    5. Two sureties to be made available during interview of administrators
